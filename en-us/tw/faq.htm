The only thing you need to do is to tell us the project and plan your company would like to work with us. We will get in touch with you as soon as possible, and make the most detailed evaluation for your company.
  What is the advantage to be the partner of Koobe®？
  Koobe® is advantaged because it is capable of research and develops, therefore we can tailor the right protection or user interface for different partners. Koobe® technology can especially protect the copyright of Koobe® PDF files and Adobe® flash files, prevent the contents from being steal, copy, printed and reproduce.
  What kind of the file should I provide？
  The two kinds of files compatible with Koobe® are Adobe® PDF file and Adobe® flash file, therefore, just provide these two kinds of files, we can then convert them into Koobe® eBook.
  What kind of digital right protection service Koobe® provide？
  The digital content that Koobe® can protect includes Adobe® PDF file and Adobe® flash animation video file format. No matter it is text, image or animation, can all be multiply-encrypted and protected.
  How much would it cost to produce an eBook？
  The cost differ form the content, therefore we need you to provide a sample of what you want to produce to estimate the cost. Koobe® would like to provide our partners the best service and the best cooperation model. Koobe® would offer different cooperation plan to different partners.


  What if the connection breaks when I download the eBook, and I fail to download the whole file？
  Koobe® does not have a resume function, if the connection breaks while you are downloading, you will need to download the Koobe® eBook again.
  Is there any limitation in download times or expiration about the eBook I downloaded？ Can I install the eBook in another computer？
  The eBook tryout we provide now does not have limitation regarding to how many times you download it. If your file or your computer failed, you can download it from our website again.
You can click on the “more information” button of each book >> [DRM] to learn about the authority limits about reading of each book.
You can install the eBook in other computer, but there is a limitation on how many computers you can install, and this is decided by the content providing company. You can also learn that information form the DRM information of each book.
  Must I read the eBook/eMagazine on line?
  The first time you read the eBook/eMagizine you purchased form Koobe® , you must login Koobe® website and acquire the authorization, then you can enjoy the eBook/eMagazine off line. However, you must pay attention to the 7-day limitation in off-line reading. If exceeding 7 days, you must login Koobe® website again, or you will not be able to open your file.
  May I sent this eBook/eMagazine file to others?
  To respect Intellectual property rights, please do not send the eBook/eMagazine file to anyone. If the user allows other people to us his/her account and password in order to login Koobe® system, we will stop all rights of that account base on our service items and relevant regulation.
  Can I print out the eBook/eMagazine I purchased?
  This depends on the authorize condition each publishing company had set. Therefore, you must pay attention to the products’ relevant information before you purchase.
